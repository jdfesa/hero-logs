# Glossary

## HeroLogs

The Android app being developed in this repository.

## LifeBar

The daily score view that summarizes the user's day through wellbeing dimensions.

## Life Score

A composite score derived from the available daily dimensions.

## Dimension

One wellbeing category shown in the LifeBar. Initial dimensions are Vitality, Joy, Drive, Calm, Connection, and Meaning.

## Timeline

The reconstructed sequence of places, trips, and meaningful day blocks.

## Visit

A time window where the user appears to remain at a place.

## Trip

A movement window between places.

## Place

A known or unknown location cluster. Users may name or categorize places.

## Signal

Any permitted device or user input used to reconstruct the day or calculate scores.

Examples:

- Location.
- Activity recognition.
- Steps.
- Sleep.
- Check-ins.

## Check-In

An optional prompt answered by the user to add subjective context.

## Baseline

The user's recent personal normal, used to compare today's signals.

## Confidence

How certain the app is about a timeline item or score.

## Local-First

A design approach where the device is the source of truth and the app does not depend on a server for core functionality.
